Civil Service Exam Practice Test

Created by: CSE Learning Essentials

Direction: Solve the following mathematical problems.

1) Jose answered all 25 test questions he was given. His percent score was taken by giving him 4 points for every correct answer and by subtracting 1 point for every wrong answer he made. Assuming he obtained a score of 70%, how many questions did he get correctly?

A) 17
B) 19
C) 15
D) 20
E) 22

2) How much does a retail merchant pay for a jacket priced at ₱1,200.00 with a single discount of 20% and then an additional 15%?

A) ₱816.00
B) ₱960.00
C) ₱1,050.00
D) ₱1,080.00
E) ₱1,104.00

3) The width of a rectangular garden is y meters. If the length is 3 meters longer than the width, what is the area if y=8?

A) 48
B) 50
C) 55
D) 88
E) 64

4) In how many ways can a manager assign 4 employees to 3 different shifts?

A) 8 ways
B) 12 ways
C) 60 ways
D) 48 ways
E) 24 ways

5) Sarah can paint a room in 5 hours. Mark can paint the same room in 6 hours. If they work together, how long will it take them to paint the room?

A) 1 hour
B) 2 hours
C) 2 5/11 hours
D) 2 1/2 hours
E) 2 8/11 hours

6) The price of shirt is increased by 15% and then reduced by 15%. The final price of the shirt is _____ of the original price.

A) the same
B) 30%
C) 2.75%
D) 97.75%
E) 70%

7) Suppose that time now is 10:30 AM. What will be the time 16 hours from now?

A) 12:30 AM
B) 2:30 AM
C) 1:30 AM
D) 12:30 PM
E) 2:30 PM

8) A factory has 8 identical machines that produce the same number of widgets at the same rate. The 8 machines complete the required number of widgets in 4 hours. The manager adds 32 more machines to meet the deadline. How many hours will it take for 40 machines to produce the same number of widgets?

A) 16 minutes
B) 20 minutes
C) 48 minutes
D) 0.75 hours
E) 20 hours

9) James put 1/3 of his stamps collection in one book and 1/2 of it in another book. He had 9 stamps left over. How many stamps does he have together?

A) 9
B) 13
C) 45
D) 54
E) 81

10) The sum of Edward’s age and Maria‘s age is 60. Nine years ago, Edward was twice as old as Maria then. How old is Maria?

A) 23 years old
B) 40 years old
C) 31 years old
D) 35 years old
E) 20 years old
