SECTION 3: NUMERICAL ABILITY - PART 3: Word Problems

PRACTICE TEST

PASSING SCORE: 8

Direction: Solve the following mathematical problems.

1) Maria has ₱120.00, Liza has ₱180.00, and Ana has ₱40.00 more than the combined amount of Maria and Liza. What is the total amount of money the three of them have?

A) ₱340.00
B) ₱500.00
C) ₱640.00
D) ₱780.00

2) A health team is monitoring the number of vaccinated individuals in two municipalities. The first municipality has 9,258 residents, while the second has 7,143. If 3,275 residents have already been vaccinated, how many are still left to be vaccinated?

A) 16,401
B) 13,126
C) 11,324
D) 12,523

3) Mr. Santos spent 4 1/2 hours checking essays from Section A and 3 1/4 hours checking essays from Section B. How much longer did it take him to check Section A than Section B?

A) 1 1/4 hours
B) 1 1/2 hours
C) 1 3/4 hours
D) 2 hours

4) Carlo sliced a pizza into 16 equal pieces to share with his cousins. He gave 5 slices to Marco and 6 slices to Anna. What fraction of the pizza did he give to his cousins altogether, and what fraction does he have left?

A) (Q1) 11/16, (Q2) 7/16
B) (Q1) 11/16, (Q2) 5/16
C) (Q1) 5/16, (Q2) 11/16
D) (Q1) 6/16, (Q2) 10/16

5) During a train promo, senior citizens pay 1/4 of the regular fare. If the regular fare is ₱36.00, how much does a senior citizen pay?

A) ₱8.00
B) ₱9.00
C) ₱10.50
D) ₱12.00

6) The average weight of three girls—Marie, Liza, and Nina—is 54 1/2 kilograms. If Marie weighs 55 1/4 kg and Liza weighs 53 3/4 kg, what is the weight of Nina?

A) 52 1/4 kg
B) 53 1/4 kg
C) 54 1/2 kg
D) 55 1/2 kg

7) In preparation for a school outreach program, 52 Grade 6 students donated ₱25.00 each, while 68 Grade 5 students donated ₱30.00 each. How much was collected from all the students?

A) ₱3,390.00
B) ₱3,740.00
C) ₱3,610.00
D) ₱3,340.00

8) In one weekend, Mr. Goku harvested 360 oranges from his orchard. He sold half of them (large oranges) at ₱20.00 each, and the other half (small oranges) at ₱10.00 each. How much did he earn from selling all the oranges?

A) ₱5,400.00
B) ₱4,800.00
C) ₱3,600.00
D) ₱10,800.00

9) A factory worker can assemble 45 electronic parts in 1 hour. How many hours will it take him to assemble 2,250 parts?

A) 50 hours
B) 45 hours
C) 40 hours
D) 35 hours

10) Mr. Angeles sells square tiles of all sizes. He packs them in cartons. The base of one of the cartons measures 16 cm by 28 cm. What are the dimensions of the largest square tile that can completely cover the base?

A) 28-cm square tile
B) 16-cm square tile
C) 8-cm square tile
D) 4-cm square tile
